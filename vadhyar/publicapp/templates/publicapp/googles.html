GOOGLE SKETCH UP

The SketchUp software is developed by Trimble.inc for people who want to study building design purposes such as architectural ,interior design , landscape architecture, civil engineering.


About The Course

The objective of the course is to equip students to use SketchUp for the model building to create ideas and present them on time, Use v-ray for SketchUp to create renderings with proper lighting and photorealism. And learn the methods to use SketchUp layouts to design presentations including the renders, floor plans, sections and elevations in an arranged way. SketchUp enables designers and architects to design shapes and lines. It allows moving the surfaces back and forth for transforming them into 3D forms fast and correctly. With the SketchUp, you will be capable of playing and creating anything that you like or desire. The ability of SketchUp is spread to the representations of the model using LayOut. The option of LayOut in SketchUp helps in applying drawing scales, adding model views to pages, and insert graphics, callouts, and dimensions. The corrections made to the SketchUp model displays automatically in LayOut which helps to save time, effort, and energy.
