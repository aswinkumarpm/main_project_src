REVIT ARCHITECTURE

Autodesk Revit is building information modelling (BIM) software developed by Autodesk for Architects, Designers,MEP Engineers, Structural Engineers, Civil Engineers.


About The Course

Autodesk Revit is building data modelling (BIM) software packages for architects, designers, Mechanical, electrical, and plumbing (MEP) engineers, structural engineers, landscape architects, and contractors. In this course, you'll acquire the ways to form structures victimizing the building information modelling (BIM) software in Revit Architecture. you'll study to operate the user interface, model the field of study parts like windows, walls, roofs, floors, doors, stairs, structure, and curtain wall system. you'll be ready to demonstrate and generate section, dimension drawings, details and schedules. You will design three-dimensional models and renderings of structures. The course covers almost the basics of Revit design, from through construction documentation. Trainees are introduced to the ideas of Building data Modelling (BIM)
